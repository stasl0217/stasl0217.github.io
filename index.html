Biography

I am a research scientist at Meta, working on recommendation systems. Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at UCLA, where I worked with Prof. Carlo Zaniolo and Prof. Yizhou Sun. My research interests include natural language processing and graph. Before joining UCLA as a Ph.D. student, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in June 2016 from Special Class for the Gifted Young at Xi'an Jiao Tong University.
